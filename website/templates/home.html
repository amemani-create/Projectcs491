About Us

Kokedama ( 玉, in English, literally "moss ball") is a ball of soil, covered with moss, on which an ornamental plant grows. (From Wikipedia) “K” of “ Kokedama” is replaced by “C” – Cokedama - “Cokedama”

It's modern, sleek, natural, and unpretentious. It's kokedama. From the Japanese, loosely translated, 'koke' means moss and 'dama' means ball. This is a free-form variant of traditional bonsai that is easy to try at home. Kokedama uses moss and string to contain plant roots, allowing plants to remain pot-free. Simple plants look amazing wrapped up in moss sheets and string, hung in windows, or perched in a shallow decorative dish. This is a great DIY that brings green inside.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote eco-friendly interior designing and inspire people to pursue careers working with plants while finding meaningful creative work to support them and their families financially.

Our Vision

To build a community that appreciates and values eco-friendly interior designs and to raise awareness of the importance of eco-friendly interior decorations through raising development.

Awareness

Create awareness among the professionals in the construction industry in Sri Lanka about Indoor Plantscaping and its benefits.

Education

Engage and excite students and professionals in interior designing fields, about horticulture, and careers working with plants.

Workforce Development

Increase the skilled workforce in plantscaping and develop a strong pipeline of future talent.

Partnerships

Cultivate and strengthen partnerships and collaborations to advance our mission, and to diversify and broaden the base of advocates, and supporters.

Resource Development

Create educational resources such as plant databases, researches, valuable tips, and the know-how with the community.

Empowerment

Empower creative moms who are forced to stay at home for various reasons by creating business opportunities for them.

Eco-friendly

Popularize Eco-friendly concepts in the indoor plantscaping field.
